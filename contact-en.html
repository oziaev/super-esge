Privacy Policy (Handling of Personal Information)

Regarding the personal information of customers obtained through the inquiry form, our company (hereinafter referred to as "the Company") shall handle such information appropriately based on the following policy.

■ 1. Personal Information Collected

The Company may collect the following information during the inquiry process:

  • ・Name
  • ・Email address
  • ・Phone number (optional)
  • ・Content of the inquiry
  • ・Other information entered into the form

■ 2. Purpose of Use

The collected personal information will be used for the following purposes:

  • ・Responding to and contacting you regarding your inquiry
  • ・Confirming the details of your request
  • ・Analysis for service improvement
  • ・Essential communications as necessary Personal information will not be used for any purposes other than those stated above.

■ 3. Disclosure to Third Parties

The collected personal information will not be disclosed or provided to third parties, except in the following cases:

  • ・When the individual’s consent has been obtained
  • ・When disclosure is required by law

■ 4. Management of Personal Information

The Company shall implement appropriate security measures to prevent the leakage, loss, or damage of personal information.

■ 5. Use of External Services

This site may utilize external services (such as email delivery systems or server providers) for form submissions. The Company ensures that these service providers also maintain appropriate management of information.

■ 6. Disclosure, Correction, and Deletion of Personal Information

If an individual requests the disclosure, correction, or deletion of their own personal information, the Company will respond appropriately to such requests.

■ 7. Revisions

This policy may be revised as necessary. The latest version will always be posted on this page.

■ 8. Contact Information

For inquiries regarding the handling of personal information, please contact us via the contact form.
