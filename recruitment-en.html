Sales (Account Planner / Consultant)

Propose solutions to advertisers and agencies using our proprietary DSP and programmatic advertising.

Key Responsibilities
  • New Business Development & Client Success:
    Acquiring new customers and deepening relationships with existing clients.
  • Strategy Planning:
    Developing advertising strategies for DSP, SNS, and Search (Listing) ads.
  • Analysis & Reporting:
    Creating performance simulations, improvement proposals, and reporting.
Requirements(Must)
  • B2B Sales Experience:
    1+ years of experience in corporate sales.
  • Core PC Skills:
    Proficiency in Excel (data management) and PowerPoint (presentation creation).
  • Achievement-Oriented:
    A high level of commitment to reaching targets.
Preferred Skills(Want)
  • Industry Experience:
    Working experience in ad agencies, publishers, or ad networks (reps).
  • Ad Tech Knowledge:
    Understanding of programmatic advertising (Google, Meta, DSP, etc.).
  • Logical Thinking:
    Ability to perform data-driven analysis and problem-solving.
Ideal Candidate
  • A self-starter who enjoys the fast-paced industry and keeps up with the latest trends.
    Someone who takes ownership of their clients' business growth.
